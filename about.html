About Me

Hi, I’m La Wun Shun Lett (Shun) — a final-year Computer Engineering student at Singapore Polytechnic. I enjoy building useful systems across web apps, IoT / Smart City, and data analytics.

Background

I’m originally from Myanmar. My interest in engineering started early — I was inspired by my second eldest sister through her engineering projects, electronics, and programming. That curiosity became a goal, and I moved to Singapore to grow my skills by specializing in Computer Engineering at Singapore Polytechnic.

What I’m working on now

Built a 5G-enabled AIoT door access system that performs AI-based facial recognition on live video streams from a Raspberry Pi camera. The system uses edge AI processing to validate users in real time and trigger hardware access indicators, focusing on low latency and end-to-end system integration.

I also work on secured end-to-end IoT (door entry systems), Smart City architecture, and data analysis for making decisions from real datasets.

My direction

My goal is to keep growing into roles where I can design practical solutions — combining software engineering, IoT system architecture, and data analytics — while collaborating with different people and contributing to projects that matter.
